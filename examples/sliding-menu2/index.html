    This example is a demo of sliding menu. Something like Google Plus or Facebook menu. Here menu slides in or out from left of the screen. Click on the respective icon in the navbar to see the effect.

    You would notice that all the three column have individual scroll bars. Navbar will stick to the top on scroll.
